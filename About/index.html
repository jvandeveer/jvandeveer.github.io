About Me

I believe the future of math research depends on computer science, and am dedicating the next portion of my life improving the skills I learned in my undergraduate computer science classes. I am a recent graduate taking a sabbatical from higher education to focus on exploring how I can best be of use to the world. I'm interested in practical applications of higher mathematics, such as differential geometry, linear algebra, and cryptography.
